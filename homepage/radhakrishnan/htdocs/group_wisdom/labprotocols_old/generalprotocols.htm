Stock solutions for E. coli growth and protein expression

 

Antibiotics (1000x stocks)

 

1)  Carbenicillin  (10 mL)

 

     To 1 g of Carbenicillin (stored at 4 °C; Ameresco), add UV/UF water up to 10 mL. Vortex, adjust volume to 10 ml, then filter solution using 0.22 mm filter.  Dispense into 1 mL aliquots and store at -20 °C.

 

2)  Kanamycin (10 mL)

 

     To 0.3 g of Kanamycin (stored at 4 °C; Ameresco), add UV/UF water up to 10 mL. Vortex, adjust volume to 10 mL, then filter solution using 0.22 mm filter.  Dispense into 1 mL aliquots and store at -20 °C.

 

3)  Chloramphenicol (10 mL)

 

     To 0.34 g of Chloramphenicol (stored at room temp), add ethanol up to 10 mL. Vortex, adjust volume to 10 mL, then filter solution using 0.22 mm filter.  Dispense into 1 mL aliquots and store at -20 °C.

 

 

1 M IPTG (1000x stock reagent for inducing protein expression) (10 mL)

 

To 2.4 g of solid IPTG (stored at 4°C; Diagnostic Chemicals), add UV/UF water up to 10 mL. Vortex, adjust volume to 10 ml, then filter solution using 0.22 mm filter.  Dispense into 1 mL aliquots and store at -20°C.

 

 

Protease Inhibitors:

 

1)     Leupeptin (1000x stock)

Dissolve 5 mg (1 bottle) into 500 mL of H2O 

 

2)     Pepstatin A (1000x stock)

Dissolve 5 mg (1 bottle) into 5 mL of ethanol

 

3)     PMSF (10 mM – 100x stock)

Dissolve 0.0174 g PMSF into 10 mL of isopropanol

 

Media for maintenance and propagation of E. coli

 

LB Broth (1 Liter) (for protein expression)

 

To 950 mL UV/UF water add 20 g of LB Broth Base powder (Life Technologies).  Adjust volume to 1000 mL.  Autoclave at 121 °C for 15 minutes. Cool solution to around 45°C and add appropriate antibiotic.

or

To 10 g Peptone powder (Life Technologies), 5 g Yeast Extract powder (Life Technologies) and 5 g NaCl, add enough UV/UF water to bring volume to 1000 mL.  Autoclave at 121 °C for 15 minutes.  Cool solution to around 45°C and add appropriate antibiotic.

 

SOC medium (for outgrowth after heat shock procedure during bacterial transformation)

 

To 20 g of Peptone powder (Life Technologies), 5 g of Yeast Extract powder (Life Technologies) and 0.5 g NaCl, add 950 mL UV/UF water.  Add 10 ml of 250 mM KCl solution.  Adjust the pH to 7.0 with NaOH.  Adjust the volume to 975 mL and sterilize by autoclaving at 121°C for 15 minutes.  Cool solution to room temperature.  Add 5 mL of a sterile solution of 2 M MgCl2 followed by 20 mL of a sterile solution of 18 % glucose.  Store at -80 °C in 50 mL (or less) aliquots.

 

10X M9 solution (1Liter)

 

To 68 g anhydrous Na2HPO4, 30 g anhydrous KH2PO4 and 5 g NaCl, add 950 mL UV/UF water.  Adjust volume to 1000 mL.  Store solution at room temperature.

 

Minimal media (for expressing 15N, 13C-labeled proteins)

Recipe for 1 L

Solution A

Dilute 100 mL of 10x M9 solution with 850 mL UV/UF water.  Autoclave.

 

Solution B   Protect from light!

10 mL 100X Vitamin solution (stored at -20 °C; Life Technologies)

100 mL 1 M CaCl2

500 mL 2 M MgCl2

3 g glucose (source of 13C-label if needed) (or 15 mL of 20% glucose)

2 g ammonium sulfate (source of 15N-label if needed) (or 20 mL of 10% solution)

 

Add 1mL of the appropriate 1000x antibiotic stock solution, bring volume to 50 mL and filter through a 0.22 mm filter.  Use sterile technique.

 

Mix 19 parts of Solution A with 1 part of Solution B after Solution A has cooled to room temperature.  Use sterile technique.  Always prepare fresh minimal media before use.

 

 

 

E. coli growth media containing agar.  

 

LB Agar (1Liter)

 

To 32 g of LB Agar powder, add 950 mL UV/UF water.  Adjust volume to 1000 mL.  Autoclave at 121 °C for 15 minutes.  Cool solution to around 45 °C and add appropriate antibiotic.  Pour 25-30 mL in each plate.  Use sterile technique.  Store plates at 4 °C after the agar has solidified.

 

Minimal media plates (1Liter)

 

Add 12 g of granulated Agar (Life Technologies) to Solution A (in Minimal media protocol) and autoclave at 121 °C for 15 minutes.  Cool Solution A to 45 °C and add Solution B (in Minimal media protocol).  Pour 25-30 mL in each plate.  Use sterile technique.  Store plates at 4 °C after the agar has solidified.

 

Terrific Broth plates (for plasmid preps) (1 Liter)

 

To 47 g of Terrific Broth powder (Life Technologies) and 12 g of granulated Agar (Life Technologies), add 950 mL UV/UF water.  Add 4 mL glycerol (Fisher).  Adjust volume to 1000 mL.  Autoclave at 121 °C for 15 minutes.  Cool solution to around 45 °C and add appropriate antibiotic.  Pour 25-30 mL in each plate.  Use sterile technique.  Store plates at 4 °C after the agar has solidified.

 

General stock solutions

 

0.5 M EDTA (pH 8.0)

 

Add 9.3 g of disodium EDTA dihydrate to 45 mL of UV/UF water.  Stir vigorously on a magnetic stirrer.  Adjust pH to 8.0 with 5 N NaOH.  Adjust volume to 50 mL.  Check pH.  Sterile filter using a 0.22 mm filter.  Store at 4°C.

3 M Sodium acetate (pH 5.2)

 

To 4.08 g sodium acetate trihydrate, add 9 mL UV/UF water.  Adjust pH to 5.2 with 5 N HCl.  Adjust volume to 10 mL.  Store at 4°C.

 

10 mM Tris-HCl, pH 8.5

 

To 0.24 g of Trizma base (Sigma), add 190 mL UV/UF water.  Adjust pH to 8.5 using HCl.  Adjust volume to 200 mL.  Store at 4°C.

 

1 M CaCl2 solution (10 mL)

 

To 2.2 g of solid CaCl2 hexahydrate, add UV/UF water up to 10 mL. Vortex, adjust volume to 10 mL, then filter solution using 0.22 mm filter.  Store at 4°C.

 

2 M MgCl2 solution (10 mL)

 

To 4.1 g of solid MgCl2 hexahydrate, add UV/UF water up to 10 mL.  Vortex, adjust volume to 10 mL.  Filter solution using 0.22 mm filter.  Store at 4 °C.

 

X-Gal solution (5 mL)

 

Work in the fume hood

To 100 mg of X-Gal (stored at -20°C; Life Technologies), add 5 mL Dimethylformamide.  Cover tube in aluminum foil and store at -20 °C.

 

250 mM KCl (50 mL)

 

To 0.93 g of KCl, add 45 ml UV/UF water.  Adjust volume to 50 mL. Store at 4°C.

 

Solutions for DNA Agarose Gel Electrophoresis

 

50X TAE

 

Dissolve 48.4 g of Trizma base (Sigma) into approximately 100 mL water, add 11.4 mL glacial acetic acid and 20 mL 0.5 M EDTA (pH 8.0).  Adjust volume to 200 mL.  Filter solution through a 0.22 mm filter.  Store at room temperature. 

 

Dilute to 1X TAE to pour and run gels.

 

Ethidium bromide solution

 

          Always Wear Gloves

To 100 mg of Ethidium bromide (Fisher), add 10 mL UV/UF water. Vortex solution to ensure that the dye has dissolved.  Wrap the container in aluminum foil and store at 4 °C.

 

6X Agarose gel-loading buffer

 

To 25 mg bromophenol blue (Bio-Rad) and 25 mg xylene cyanol FF (Bio-Rad), add 7 mL UV/UF water.  Add 3 mL glycerol.  Vortex solution to dissolve dye.  Cover tube with aluminum foil and store at 4 °C.

 

 

Solutions for Tris-HCl SDS-PAGE

 

1.5 M Tris-HCl, pH 8.8

To 18 g of Trizma base (Sigma), add 95 mL UV/UF water.  Adjust pH to 8.8 using 2 N HCl.  Adjust volume to 100 mL.  Store at room temperature. 

 

0.5 M Tris-HCl, pH 6.8

To 6 g of Trizma base (Sigma), add 95 mL UV/UF water.  Adjust pH to 6.8 using 5 N HCl.  Adjust volume to 100 mL.  Store at room temperature.

 

10% SDS solution

Heat 180 mL of UV/UF water to 68°C to assist dissolution. Add 20 g of electrophoresis-grade SDS (Bio-Rad; weigh carefully without spilling) and stir. When the SDS is dissolved, adjust the pH to 7.2 with 5 N HCl.  Adjust the volume to 200 mL.  Store at room temperature.

Molecular weight markers

 

Resuspend 1 vial of Low Molecular Weight Markers (Sigma M3913) in 100 mL UV/UF water.  Centrifuge vial within a 50 mL conical tube to pool all of the marker at the bottom of the vial.  Dispense into 25 mL aliquots and store at -20°C.  Use 5-10 mL for a mini-gel. 

 

Coomassie Staining solution

 

Prepare 500 mL of Destaining solution.  Add 1.25 g of Coomassie Brilliant Blue R-250 (always wear gloves).  Vigorously stir solution using a magnetic stirrer.  Filter solution through a Whatman No. 1 filter paper to remove any particulate matter.  Store solution at room temperature. 

 

Coomassie Destaining solution

 

Work in the fume hood. 

Using a 1 L graduated cylinder, add 500 mL UV/UF water, 400 mL methanol (Fisher), and 100 mL glacial acetic acid (Fisher).  Mix thoroughly by stirring.  Pour solution into BioRad destain bottles.  Store at room temperature in the flammable cabinet (under the hood).
